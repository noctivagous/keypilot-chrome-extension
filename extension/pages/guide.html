KeyPilot Guide

Keyboard-first navigation reference.

Core keys

  • F click element under the KeyPilot cursor
  • D / S back
  • R forward
  • Q / W tab left/right
  • Alt + L omnibox
  • Esc cancel / exit

Popover

  • E open link-under-cursor in popover
  • ' open Settings popover
  • Z/X/C/V/B/N scroll
  • Esc close
